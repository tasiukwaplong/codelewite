HOW IT WORKS


For students

  • Register as a student/researcher
  • Login and update your profile, seting institutions you've attended
  • Upload a copy of your project specifying a summary and title
  • Once your research is selected for sponsorship, you will be notified via email and phone number you provided during rergisteration

For agencies

  • Register as an agency/sponsor using your agencys' official email address
  • Login and update your profile, seting choice of projects you wish to sponsor and address
  • There will be a physical verification to confirm your office really exists
  • Pick a project you wish to sponsor and the researcher will get contacted.
